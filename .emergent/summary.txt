<original_problem_statement>
The user wants to build a modern, cross-platform (Android/iOS) mobile app for restaurant discovery and reservations.

**Core Features (Initial Request):**
- **Authentication**: Login and register functionality.
- **UI**: Advanced, modern UI with Montserrat font.
- **Navigation**: A bottom slider menu with acasa, restaurante, rezervari, profil, nou, and harta.
- **Maps**: Google Maps integration to search for addresses.

**Product Requirements (Added during conversation):**
- **Acasa (Home) Screen**: Display restaurants in modern blocks with photos, reviews, and sorting (popular, sponsored, liked). Sponsored restaurants appear first. Allow users to filter by food categories (Pizza, Sushi, etc.).
- **Restaurant Details**: When a restaurant is clicked, show its menu, dish photos with price/quantity, and a map popup showing its location and distance. Also, provide options to view the restaurant in 2D, 3D, or via a short video.
- **Reservations (Rezervari)**:
    - **Type 1**: Reserve a table and pre-order food. This cannot be canceled within 1 hour of the reservation time.
    - **Type 2**: Reserve a table with a small upfront fee set by the restaurant, which is deducted from the final bill.
- **Profile (Profil)**: User settings, payment information, and profile details.
- **Company Features**:
    - **Registration**: A separate registration flow for businesses (inregistreaza-te ca firma) using a CUI number (2-10 digits).
    - **Company Dashboard**: After verification, companies can upload their restaurant details, including images, product sections (e.g., aperitive, pizza), and an optional 50-word description.
    - **3D Meal Photography**: Ability for companies to photograph their meals in 3D (UI placeholder).
- **Admin Panel**:
    - An admin with the email  can monitor companies, modify their details, and add new restaurants without needing verification.
    - The panel should show new company registration requests for verification.
- **CUI Verification**: Automatic verification of the CUI number using the ANAF API.
- **Payments**: A backend system for a payment system. A 1.7% commission should be deduced from each purchase made through the app.
- **Support**:
    - A support email for both clients and companies.
    - An in-app chat assistance feature.

</original_problem_statement>
